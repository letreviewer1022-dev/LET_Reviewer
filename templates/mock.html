Mock Exam

In this section, you can test your knowledge and readiness through a comprehensive mock examination designed to simulate the actual licensure exam experience. This allows you to assess your strengths and identify areas for improvement in a timed, exam-like environment.

Mock Exam Instructions

In this section, you will undertake a full-length mock exam that mirrors the structure and content of the actual licensure examination. This practice exam is designed to help you become familiar with the format, timing, and types of questions you will encounter, allowing you to build confidence and improve your test-taking strategies.

The mock exam will have a set of 150 number of question per subject covering Professional Education, TLE Specialization, and General Education. You will have a total of 3 hours to complete the exam, simulating real exam conditions. Make sure to manage your time effectively and answer all questions to the best of your ability.
